A little about me

I am aspiring to be the best software developer I can be. That aspiration is what brought me to Galvanize, so I would gain the most important skill I could: learing how to learn. I was born and raised across the street from the Disneyland amusement park in Anaheim, California.

I enlisted in the Army at the age of 19 in 2007 as a Military Police Officer. My first assignment was in Heidelberg, Germany where I conducted Honor and Color Guard ceremonies. With a highspeed attitude, I was quickly selected for Protective Services after threatening to shoot a lady in front of former Vice President Dick Chaney. I spent a year serving as 4 star General in command of the Army in Europe.

After 2 years in Germany, I was stationed at Fort Carson in Colorado Springs, where I spent 3 years conducting strictly law enforcement. It was during that time where I discovered that I had no real interest in continuing in that field of work. Admittedly, transitioning out of the Army was significantly more difficult than I had anticipated. It has taken me just over 6 years to figure out what direction I wanted my life to move into and discovered the development industry.
